## Summary

This collaboration focused on agent models for (PO)MDPs that account for bounded rationality and human-like bias.

## Research Question

How should rational-agent models be adapted when the objective is to better reflect realistic, biased, or bounded decision-makers?

## Methodology

- Agent modeling for (PO)MDPs
- Benchmark construction in Julia
- Emphasis on computational tools for structured decision-process research

## Key Contributions

- Implemented agent models with bounded-rational behavior
- Built JuliaProgrammingPuzzles.jl to benchmark ParametricInversion.jl
- Strengthened the connection between modeling and reproducible tooling
