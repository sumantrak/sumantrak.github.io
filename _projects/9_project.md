## Summary

This project examines failure modes in AutoML pipelines where optimization pressure can amplify spurious patterns and
produce convincing but misguided conclusions.

## Research Question

How do automated model-search procedures create opportunities for unintentional hacking of the target metric or scientific claim?

## Methodology

- Empirical stress-testing of AutoML behavior
- Analysis of optimization-induced failure modes
- Framing around reliability rather than benchmark-only performance

## Key Contributions

- Studies the interaction between automated search and methodological misuse
- Highlights a systems-level failure mode rather than an isolated algorithmic bug
- Connects ML tooling with scientific reliability concerns
